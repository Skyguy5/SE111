REVIEW

Skyler Perry February 13, 2021

Glass Castle is an extremely emotional, hard hitting, amazing movie in my eyes. There are multiple scenes that tug the heart strings of the audience and make doubting the morals of any one character even that much harder to do, as the dysfunctional family travels all over the country, living off whatever they can get their hands on and making the best of it; all displayed through the eyes of various family members

Trivia

  • Jennifer Lawrence was attached to star and produce the movie for a long period of time but dropped out. Brie Larson was cast in the lead role after Lawrence dropped out.
  • 'The Crystal Castle' was based on the memoirs of journalist Jeannette Walls.
  • Brie Larson and Woody Harrelson worked together on 'Rampart'.
